◆ ReadRangeTable()

Bool_t KVedaLossMaterial::ReadRangeTable ( FILE *  fp)

Read Z- & A-dependent range parameters for material

For each material we create 4 TF1 objects: KVedaLossMaterial:[type]:Range - gives range in g/cm**2 as a function of particle energy KVedaLossMaterial:[type]:StoppingPower - gives dE/dx in MeV/(g/cm**2) as a function of particle energy KVedaLossMaterial:[type]:EnergyLoss - gives dE as a function of particle energy KVedaLossMaterial:[type]:ResidualEnergy - gives energy after material (0 if particle stops)

The TF1::fNpx parameter for these functions is defined by the environment variables

KVedaLoss.Range.Npx: 20 also used for StoppingPower KVedaLoss.EnergyLoss.Npx: 50 KVedaLoss.ResidualEnergy.Npx: 20
