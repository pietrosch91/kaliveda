◆ DistanceToMarker()

Double_t KVIDQAMarker::DistanceToMarker ( Double_t  px,
Double_t  py 
) const

Computes the closest distance of approach from point (px,py) to this marker. This distance is calculated on a A/Q scale i.e. if the Y coordinate is A then the Y coordinate will be divide by GetQ(). If the Y coordinate is Q then Y is change by GetA()/Y. Keep in mind that the X coordinate is supposed to give the ratio A/Q (mass number over the charge state).
