11 /*
12 <h2>KVZMax</h2>
13 <h4>Global variable used to sort particles in order of decreasing atomic number</h4>
14 
15  Global variable returning the charge of the `(i+1)`-th heaviest fragment
16  and the pointer to the `(i+1)`-th heaviest fragment, where `i=0,1,...,M-1`
17  with `M =` total multiplicity of event.
18 
19  All nuclei of the event are sorted in descending order according to their charge
20  (`Z(0)` > `Z(1)` > `Z(2)` > ... > `Z(M-1)`) .
21 
22  Simple interface GetValue() returns Z of heaviest fragment
23  and GetObject returns pointer to this fragment.
24 
25  All other fragments accessible via GetValue(i) and GetZmax(i), `i` ranging
26  from 0 to `M-1` (`M`=total multiplicity)
27  (the latter method returns a KVNucleus pointer directly).
28 
29  Also GetValue(`"Zmax1"`), GetValue(`"Zmax2"`), ..., GetValue(`"Zmax50"`)
30  can be used to obtain Z of 50 heaviest nuclei.
31 
32  By default when used in a KVGVList global variable list and automatic TTree branch creation is used
33  (see method KVGVList::MakeBranches and the example given below), the first 3 largest Z will be stored.
34  You can change this with method SetMaxNumBranches().
35 */
