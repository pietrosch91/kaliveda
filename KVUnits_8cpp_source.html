39 If x is a quantity expressed in terms of one of the standard units, the
40 corresponding quantity in terms of a different unit is obtained by
41 dividing x by the appropriate numerical constant:<br>
42 <br>

44 (x/KVUnits::mbar) is pressure in millibar;<br>

46 x = length in centimetres: (x/KVUnits::um) is length in
47 microns/micrometres.<br>
48 </div>

50 On the other hand, if x is a quantity expressed in arbitrary units,
51 then in order to express it in terms of standard units multiply x by
52 the appropriate numerical constant:<br>
53 <br>
54 <div style="margin-left: 40px;">x = pressure in atmospheres:
55 (x*KVUnits::atm) is pressure in torr;<br>
