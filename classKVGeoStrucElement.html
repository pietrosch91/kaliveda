void KVGeoStrucElement::Add ( KVBase *  element)
virtual

Add a detector or a daughter structure to this structure. Note that daughter structures are by defeault owned by their mother and will be deleted by her destructor. Call SetOwnsDaughters(kFALSE) to change. Detectors are not owned by default - call SetOwnsDetectors(kTRUE) to change.

Reimplemented in KVTelescope, KVRing, and KVASGroup.
