KVZboundAv Class Reference
The Globvars module

Similar to KVZbound but the sum is computed for fragments and particles with a positive parallel velocity, Vz>0, in the reference frame defined by a call to SetFrame() ("CM" frame by default).

====> Example: compute the sum of the particles and fragment with a charge greater than or equal to 5 and Vz > Vcm.

... KVZboundAv *zb5av=new KVZboundAv("zbound5Av"); zb5av->SetFrame("CM"); zb5av->SetZmin(5);

KVGVList *gvlist=new KVGVList(); // (or KVGVList *gvlist=GetGVList(); if in a KVSelector) gvlist->Add(zb5av); ...

Look at KVVarGlob class to have an example of use.
